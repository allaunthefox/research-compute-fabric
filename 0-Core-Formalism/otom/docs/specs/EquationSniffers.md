# Equation Sniffers

Status: READY_FOR_SPEC_MODULE

## Definition

An Equation Sniffer is a software-only resonance probe for the equation forest.

It does not patrol, pursue, enforce, surveil, or act in the physical world. It follows mathematical scent trails: structural resonances, witness hierarchies, residual motifs, adapter candidates, and provenance paths.

## Core purpose

Equation Sniffers automate bounded exploration of mathematical terrain without implying physical autonomy.

They operate on:

- equation graphs
- document manifolds
- witness hierarchies
- residual fields
- behavioral manifolds
- route candidates
- provenance trails

They do not operate as drones, guards, patrol agents, or enforcement systems.

## Canonical law

An Equation Sniffer does not patrol the forest. It smells for structure.

Short form:

It follows mathematical scent, not targets.

## Allowed actions

- probe candidate equation regions
- extract witness hierarchy
- compare residuals
- detect repeated operator shapes
- tag possible adapters
- suggest routes
- record provenance
- emit route candidates for review
- hand stable witnesses to BHOCS
- hand unresolved residuals to FAMM

## Forbidden actions

- physical actuation
- surveillance
- pursuit
- enforcement
- weaponization
- autonomous real-world intervention
- target tracking
- patrol behavior

## Relation to Field-Native Witness Hierarchy

Equation Sniffers consume witness packets produced by the Field-Native Witness Hierarchy.

A witness packet provides:

```text
basis + witness coordinate + amplitude + phase + action + residual receipt
```

The Equation Sniffer uses that packet to compare the current region against known scent trails:

```text
current witness hierarchy
-> compare against known route signatures
-> rank adapter candidates
-> log unresolved residuals
-> suggest next probe
```

## Claim boundaries

- Equation Sniffers are software-only analysis agents.
- They are not physical drones.
- They do not imply patrol, pursuit, surveillance, or enforcement.
- They provide route suggestions and provenance records, not autonomous real-world action.
- A sniffer result is a candidate scent trail, not a proof unless attached evidence or formal verification exists.

## One-line summary

Equation Sniffers are bounded software probes that smell for reusable mathematical structure in the equation forest and report routes without acting outside the analysis substrate.
