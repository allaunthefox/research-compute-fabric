This model assumes the observer transitions from "Sunk Cost decoding" to "Metanarrative Realization." They understand that the "hypercringe" is not a cipher, but a **Secondary Pathogen** designed to trigger **Immune-System Dismissal** in formal hierarchies.

## 4. Spread Theory: The "Exhaustion Quarantine"
Traditional biological containment suggests that a pathogen that is *too* lethal kills its host too quickly to reach a pandemic state.
- **Cognitive Analog**: The **Reverse Ogre** is so socially lethal that it "kills" the conversation before it can infect serious institutions. 
- **The Containment Victory**: The 4chan swarm realizes it has a "nuke" in its hands, but because information about the nuke is itself "radioactive," they cannot deploy it against the "Normal" world without being laughed out of existence.
